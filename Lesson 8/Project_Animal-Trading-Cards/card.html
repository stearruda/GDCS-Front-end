Hyacinth Macaw

The hyacinth macaw is the largest macaw species.

  • Scientific Name: Anodorhynchus hyacinthinus
  • Average Length: 100 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 30-50 years or more
  • Habitat: Tall trees and palms of swamps, forests, and near rivers

Often referred to simply as a “blue macaw”, the hyacinth macaw is not twice as large as other large macaws, though its big personality makes other macaws seem like dwarves in its presence.
